About Our Training Gallery

Welcome to the USS Academy Training Gallery, a visual record of more than 27 years of firearms education delivered across the state of Florida. Every photograph in this collection was taken during real classes with real students. Nothing here is staged, stock, or simulated. What you see is what our instructors deliver every week: structured, professional firearms training that meets the highest standards in the industry. From first-time shooters earning their Florida Concealed Weapons Permit to experienced marksmen refining advanced tactical skills, this gallery captures the full spectrum of what USS Academy offers.

The gallery currently features more than 175 photographs organized across multiple training categories. You will find images from our popular CWP classes, where students learn safe firearms handling, Florida self-defense law, and complete their live-fire qualification. You will also see photos from our tactical training programs, including vehicle engagement drills, barricade shooting, and movement-based exercises that prepare students for real-world defensive scenarios. Our night vision courses are represented here as well, showcasing students operating under low-light and no-light conditions with helmet-mounted NVGs, IR aiming devices, and suppressed weapon systems.

Beyond the range, this gallery documents our classroom instruction sessions where students receive in-depth education on Florida firearms law, use-of-force principles, situational awareness, and the legal responsibilities that come with carrying a concealed weapon. You will also find outdoor range sessions at our partner facilities throughout Central Florida, competitive shooting events, and our dedicated women's training programs. Every image tells a story of discipline, growth, and commitment to responsible firearms ownership.

Training Categories Featured in This Gallery

The photographs in our gallery span every major training program USS Academy offers. Below is a breakdown of the categories you will find represented throughout the collection.

Beginner & CWP Classes

Our most popular program. Students learn safe firearms handling, Florida concealed carry law, and complete their live-fire qualification. Classes are held at indoor and outdoor ranges across Central Florida with groups ranging from six to thirty students per session.

Tactical Operations

Advanced training that includes vehicle engagement, barricade shooting, prone position drills, and team-based movement exercises. These courses are designed for students who want to move beyond basic marksmanship into defensive and tactical applications.

Night Vision Training

Our elite night vision course teaches students to operate under low-light and no-light conditions using helmet-mounted NVGs, IR lasers, thermal optics, and suppressed weapon platforms. One of the most unique training experiences available in Florida.

Classroom Instruction

Every course at USS Academy includes a classroom component covering Florida firearms law, use-of-force principles, situational awareness, and the legal responsibilities of carrying a concealed weapon. Our instructors bring decades of law enforcement and military experience to every lecture.

Outdoor Range Sessions

Live-fire training at outdoor ranges across Florida. Students practice pistol and rifle marksmanship, draw-from-holster drills, target transitions, and timed qualification courses under the supervision of certified instructors at partner facilities throughout the state.

Competitive Shooting

Photos from USPSA, IDPA, and steel challenge events. USS Academy prepares students for competitive shooting through structured drills, stage planning, and match-day coaching. Competition builds speed, accuracy, and confidence under pressure.

Women's Training

USS Academy is proud to offer dedicated training programs for women, including our Ember & Iron ladies-only shooting events. These sessions provide a supportive, judgment-free environment where women can build confidence, learn self-defense fundamentals, and connect with a community of like-minded individuals.

Why USS Academy

USS Academy has been training responsible firearms owners in the state of Florida for more than 27 years. In that time, we have trained over 29,000 students across every skill level, from complete beginners who have never touched a firearm to law enforcement professionals and military veterans seeking advanced tactical certification. Our longevity in this industry is not an accident. It is the result of consistent quality, professional instruction, and an unwavering commitment to student safety and education.

Every instructor at USS Academy holds active credentials from one or more of the following organizations: the National Rifle Association (NRA), the United States Concealed Carry Association (USCCA), the United States Marine Corps (USMC), and Florida law enforcement agencies. Several of our lead instructors hold Florida K Licenses and have direct experience in armed security, executive protection, and tactical operations. When you train with USS Academy, you are learning from professionals who have applied these skills in the field, not just in a classroom.

We operate at partner ranges across the state of Florida, from Central Florida facilities near Orlando to locations serving Tampa Bay, the Space Coast, South Florida, Northeast Florida, and the Panhandle. This network of partner ranges allows us to bring professional firearms training to communities throughout the state without requiring students to travel long distances. Whether you are in Jacksonville, Miami, Pensacola, or anywhere in between, USS Academy can deliver world-class training close to home. Our classes run year-round, with weekly sessions in Central Florida and scheduled events in every major region of the state. Class sizes are intentionally kept small to ensure every student receives personalized attention and hands-on instruction from our certified team.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit my own training photos to the gallery?

Yes. USS Academy alumni and current students are welcome to submit photos from their training experience. Visit our Photo Submission page to upload your images. All submissions are reviewed by our team before being added to the gallery. We love seeing our students' progress and are proud to showcase the community we have built over 27 years.

Can I use these photos for personal or commercial purposes?

All photographs in the USS Academy Training Gallery are the property of USS Academy and may not be reproduced, distributed, or used for commercial purposes without prior written permission. If you appear in a photo and would like a copy for personal use, or if you are a media outlet seeking permission to publish, please contact us at support@ussacademy.org or call 800-508-8772.

How often is the gallery updated?

We add new photos regularly as classes are held throughout the year. Our instructors document training sessions across all programs, and new images are uploaded after each major class cycle. Training videos are also in production and will be added to the Videos tab once available on our YouTube channel.

I am in one of these photos and would like it removed. How do I request that?

We respect your privacy. If you appear in a gallery photo and would like it removed, please email support@ussacademy.org with a description or screenshot of the image in question. We will remove it within 48 hours of receiving your request. All students sign a media release waiver during registration, but we honor removal requests without question.
