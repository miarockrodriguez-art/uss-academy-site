Requirements

Florida CWP Requirements

You must be 21 or older (18 with qualifying military service), a U.S. citizen or lawful permanent resident, legally able to possess a firearm, and able to demonstrate competency through a certified training course with live-fire.

Florida is now a permitless-carry state. The CWP is still strongly recommended because it provides reciprocity with 37+ other states, satisfies the federal NICS background check at point of purchase, provides specific legal exemptions (school pickup vehicle carry, etc.), and demonstrates completed training — a material factor in self-defense legal outcomes.
