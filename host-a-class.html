Requirements

Venue Requirements

USS Academy works with a wide variety of venues. Here is what we need from your space to deliver a safe, professional training experience.

Classroom-Only Classes

For lecture-based courses (Self-Defense Seminars, Open Carry Navigation, Safe Storage), we need a room that comfortably seats your expected group with chairs, a projection screen or large TV, and access to power outlets. HOA clubhouses, church fellowship halls, gym studios, and conference rooms all work. Restroom access and parking for students are the only other requirements.

Live-Fire Classes

For courses with a range component (CWP, Beginner Courses, Tactical Training), a licensed shooting range facility is required. The range must have adequate lane space, proper backstops, ventilation (indoor), and meet all local and state safety regulations. USS Academy carries full general liability insurance and can add your facility as additional insured at no cost.

Minimum Student Count & Group Sizes

Hosted classes require a minimum of 10 students to schedule. Maximum capacity depends on your venue and the course type. Classroom-only courses can accommodate up to 100 students in the right space. Live-fire classes are capped based on available range lanes and instructor-to-student ratios for safety. USS Academy provides all instructional materials, printed handouts, certificates of completion, and course supplies. For live-fire classes, we provide rental firearms and ammunition for students who do not have their own.

Previous Host Locations

USS Academy has hosted classes at shooting ranges, HOA community centers, churches, corporate offices, hotel conference rooms, fitness centers, and private estates across Central Florida and beyond. Our travel model means we bring the same quality of instruction to every location — whether it is a 200-seat hotel ballroom in Orlando or a 15-person classroom at a neighborhood clubhouse in Sanford. Over 29,000 students have trained through our programs at dozens of partner venues across the state of Florida.

Questions

Host a Class FAQ

Can USS Academy hold firearms training at my location?

Yes. USS Academy partners with ranges, gyms, HOAs, churches, corporate campuses, and private venues across Florida to bring CWP classes and firearms training directly to your community. For classroom-only courses, any room with seating and a screen works. For live-fire, a licensed range facility is required.

What does USS Academy need from the host venue?

We need a safe indoor or outdoor space, adequate lighting, and access to restrooms. For live-fire classes, a licensed range facility is required. We handle all instruction, materials, certificates, and logistics. You provide the space and the students.

How many students can attend a hosted class?

Minimum 10 students per class. Maximum capacity ranges from 35 for range-based courses to 100 or more for classroom-only seminars, depending on your venue size. We customize group sizes to fit your space and audience safely.

Is there a cost to the host venue?

No. USS Academy covers all instruction costs, materials, insurance, and certificates. Revenue-sharing partnerships are available for recurring classes. Contact us at 407-305-8335 to discuss partnership options.

Can I host a class for my employees as team building?

Absolutely. Corporate team-building events are among our most popular hosted programs. Firearms safety and marksmanship training builds teamwork, communication, focus, and confidence. We customize the curriculum for corporate groups to emphasize fundamentals and positive experience. No prior firearms experience required for participants.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3 weeks in advance to allow time for promotion and student registration. For large events (50+ students) or recurring partnerships, 4-6 weeks is ideal. Rush scheduling may be available depending on instructor availability. Call 407-305-8335 to check dates.
