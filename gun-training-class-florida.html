USS Academy delivers the complete gun training curriculum in Florida — from a first-time shooter's introduction through concealed carry, tactical, precision, night vision, and professional instructor certification. 29,000 students trained across 27 years.

Overview

Complete Gun Training for Florida Students

USS Academy has been the state of Florida's go-to firearms training academy since 1997. What began as a single concealed carry class has grown into the most complete civilian gun training curriculum in the southeastern United States — six tiers covering beginner handling through professional instructor certification, with every class taught in person by a certified instructor on a real range. 29,000 students have trained through USS Academy programs over 27 years.

Our gun training classes are structured so every student, regardless of starting experience, has a clear path. Beginners start at Tier 1 with foundational safety and handling. CWP applicants take the Tier 1 concealed carry class and leave with a state-approved certificate the same day. Intermediate and advanced students move through Tiers 2 through 5 covering defensive shooting, tactical training, precision marksmanship, and night vision operations. Future instructors enroll in Tier 6 for professional development.

Statewide Coverage

Training Available Across Florida

USS Academy operates from a network of partner ranges across Florida. Our primary training location is 595 Donovan Crews Road, Saint Cloud, FL 34773, serving the Central Florida corridor. Additional partner ranges across Tampa Bay, Northeast Florida, Northwest Florida, South Florida, and the Space Coast activate seasonally based on demand.

For students outside the Central Florida corridor, we offer regional training pages covering the specific class schedules and partner ranges in each area: Central Florida, Tampa Bay, Northeast Florida, and more at Service Areas.

Instructors

Credentialed Professionals, Real Operational Experience

Every USS Academy class is led by an instructor with verifiable credentials. Our roster includes active and retired law enforcement officers, United States Marine Corps veterans, NRA-certified range safety officers and instructors, USCCA-certified instructors, and Florida K License holders. This is not a roster of internet-certified hobbyists. Every instructor has operational field experience backing up the credential.

Classes are structured to maximize student learning. Instructor-to-student ratios are capped based on the course type. Every range session has supervision at every firing position. Every classroom session is interactive and structured around a proven teaching methodology refined over 27 years and 29,000+ students.

Why Train With Us

Operating Standards That Define the Program

USS Academy has built its reputation on one principle: you do not learn to handle a firearm from a book or a video. You learn by handling the firearm, under instruction, on a range, with an instructor who knows what you are doing right and wrong.

  • Live-fire on partner ranges. Licensed, insured, supervised. Not a parking lot. Not a simulator.
  • Small class sizes. Classroom-range format with capped student counts for real instructor attention.
  • State-approved certificates. CWP students leave with the documentation to apply through FDACS.
  • Rental firearms available. Try before you buy, or train without owning yet.
  • Private and group options. One-on-one sessions, family bookings, corporate teams.
  • Hosted classes. We can bring training to your range, HOA, or campus. See Host a Class.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of gun training classes does USS Academy offer?

USS Academy offers a complete six-tier curriculum covering beginner handling, concealed carry, defensive shooting, tactical training, precision marksmanship, night vision, and instructor certification.

Do I need prior experience for a gun training class?

No. Every USS Academy program has an entry point for beginners. Our Tier 1 classes are specifically designed for students with no prior firearm experience.

Where are your gun training classes held in Florida?

Our primary training location is in Saint Cloud, FL. Additional partner ranges across the state are activated seasonally based on demand. See our Service Areas for a full coverage map.

Are your instructors certified?

Yes. Every USS Academy instructor holds credentials from the NRA, USCCA, U.S. Marine Corps, Florida Class K License, or Law Enforcement Firearms Instructor programs.

Can I bring my own firearm?

Yes. Rental firearms are also available for students who do not yet own a firearm or prefer not to transport theirs.

Do you offer private or group training?

Yes. Private one-on-one sessions, family bookings, and corporate team training are scheduled on request. Group pricing applies at 6 or more students.
