Your Home for Firearms Excellence in Central Florida

Central Florida is where USS Academy was born, and it remains the epicenter of everything we do. For over twenty-seven years, our instructors have walked into partner ranges across the Orlando metro and surrounding communities to deliver training that changes lives. From the new gun owner in Winter Garden who wants to feel safe at home, to the Kissimmee professional preparing for a career in law enforcement, our classrooms are filled with Central Floridians who chose substance over shortcuts.

What makes our Central Florida program different is accessibility. We hold more classes here than anywhere else in the state, at partner ranges strategically located so residents from Sanford to Clermont, Apopka to Lake Mary, never have to drive far to find world-class instruction. Our flagship CWP permit courses run multiple times per month, and our advanced tactical and night vision programs fill consistently because students in the I-4 corridor understand the difference between a certificate and genuine competence.

Orlando is a city of transplants, tourists, and growth. That means the personal security landscape here is unlike anywhere else in Florida. Our curriculum reflects this reality. We train students to navigate crowded environments, understand situational awareness in high-traffic areas, and build the reflexive decision-making skills that matter when seconds count. Residents from Deltona and Ocoee who commute into Orlando daily tell us that the situational awareness modules alone were worth the enrollment.

Our partnership with local ranges across Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Lake, and Volusia counties means you get hands-on trigger time in professional facilities without the overhead of a single fixed location. Whether you are starting with the fundamentals or advancing into precision marksmanship and low-light engagement, Central Florida students enjoy the most frequent class schedules and the shortest waitlists of any region we serve. This is our home turf, and it shows.

Why Central Florida Residents Choose USS Academy

Central Florida residents have more firearms training options than any other part of the state, yet thousands choose USS Academy year after year. The reason is straightforward: our instructors deliver measurable skill development rather than checkbox compliance. Every class is led by professionals with verified credentials from law enforcement agencies, the United States Marine Corps, the NRA, and the USCCA. Students in Orlando, Kissimmee, Winter Garden, and surrounding communities know the difference between an instructor who reads from a script and one who has spent decades on the range refining their teaching methodology through real-world experience.

Our default class location at Triple N Ranch, 595 Donovan Crews Rd, Saint Cloud FL 34773, provides a controlled outdoor range environment with ample space for both classroom and live-fire instruction. The facility is easily accessible from the I-4 corridor, the 417 toll road, and major routes from Kissimmee, Orlando, and the greater Osceola County area. For students traveling from northern Orange County communities like Apopka and Lake Mary, the drive is typically under 45 minutes. Weekend and weekday scheduling ensures you can find a class date that fits your work schedule.

Instructor availability in Central Florida is the strongest of any region we serve. Because this is our home base, our lead instructors are available for classes multiple days per week. Private one-on-one sessions, group bookings for corporate teams, and recurring Bullseye Benefactor membership sessions are all scheduled with regularity that other regions cannot match. Central Florida students also gain priority access to our advanced Tier 3 through Tier 5 courses, which require specialized range setups that are most frequently available at our primary training facilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about firearms training in the Central Florida region

Where does USS Academy hold classes in Central Florida?

Our primary training location is Triple N Ranch at 595 Donovan Crews Rd, Saint Cloud FL 34773. We also hold classes at partner ranges across Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Lake, and Volusia counties. Hotel-based classroom seminars are conducted at venues in the greater Orlando area. Call 407-305-8335 for your nearest upcoming class location.

How much does a CWP class cost in Orlando?

CWP classes at USS Academy start at $99 for beginner courses. Full concealed weapons permit classes with live-fire qualification range from $149 to $179 per person. Group, semi-private, and private session options are available at adjusted pricing. Call 407-305-8335 for current rates and availability.

Do you offer weekend and evening classes?

Yes. USS Academy holds CWP classes and firearms training on weekdays, evenings, and weekends throughout Central Florida. Saturday classes are the most popular and fill first. We recommend booking at least one week in advance to guarantee your preferred date. Check our class calendar or call 407-305-8335 to book.

Can I take a firearms class with no experience?

Absolutely. Our Entry-Level Beginner Course and Basic Firearm Safety Course are specifically designed for first-time gun owners. No prior experience is required. USS Academy provides rental firearms, ammunition, and all safety equipment. Our instructors are patient, professional, and experienced at teaching students who have never touched a firearm before.
