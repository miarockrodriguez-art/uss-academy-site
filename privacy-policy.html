Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

USS Academy LLC ("USS Academy," "we," "us") collects the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, date of birth, and state-issued ID number when provided for class registration, waiver signing, or certificate processing.
  • Payment Information: Credit card and billing information processed through our secure payment processors (Stripe, Square). We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage Information: Website browsing data, IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs collected through standard web analytics.
  • Communications: Records of emails, SMS messages, phone calls, and form submissions you send to or receive from us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process class registrations and payments
  • Issue training certificates and track permit renewals
  • Send booking confirmations, reminders, and follow-up communications
  • Provide customer support
  • Send marketing communications about classes, events, and promotions (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations including Florida firearms training documentation requirements
  • Improve our services and website

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and assist with marketing efforts. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site functionality.

We may use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for site functionality such as form submissions and navigation.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertising and track campaign performance.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Stripe: Payment processing — stripe.com/privacy
  • Square: Payment processing — squareup.com/legal/privacy
  • GoHighLevel: CRM, SMS, and email communications
  • Google Analytics: Website traffic analysis
  • Cloudflare: Website hosting and security
  • WaiverForever: Digital waiver collection and storage

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

5. SMS and Text Message Policy

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive text messages from USS Academy including booking confirmations, class reminders, schedule updates, and promotional offers. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe from marketing messages. Reply HELP for assistance. We honor all opt-out requests within 10 business days as required by the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA).

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS), secure payment processing, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

  • Training records and certificates: Retained indefinitely as required for permit documentation.
  • Contact information: Retained for the duration of our business relationship and up to 4 years after last contact for compliance purposes.
  • Payment records: Retained as required by tax and financial regulations.
  • Marketing consent records: Retained for 4 years as required by TCPA.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. Minors may participate in certain programs with parental or guardian consent and supervision as outlined in our waiver.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
