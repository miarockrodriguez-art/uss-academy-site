Our Mission

Why USS Academy Trains Statewide

USS Academy has been at the forefront of firearms education in Florida for over 27 years. What started as a local concealed weapons permit class in Central Florida has grown into one of the most recognized firearms training programs in the southeastern United States. With more than 29,000 students trained across every region of the state, USS Academy has built a reputation for delivering safe, professional, and accessible firearms instruction to civilians, law enforcement professionals, military personnel, and corporate security teams alike.

Our ability to serve students statewide comes from a growing network of partner ranges and training facilities across all of Florida's major metropolitan areas and rural communities. From the military-heavy Panhandle region near Pensacola and Eglin Air Force Base, through the rapidly growing Tampa Bay corridor, down to the densely populated South Florida metro — USS Academy has established training relationships that allow us to bring our curriculum directly to the communities that need it most.

In addition to our in-person training at partner ranges across the state, USS Academy offers online and virtual classroom options that extend our reach beyond Florida's borders. Students from anywhere in the United States can access our foundational safety courses, concealed carry law education, and classroom-based portions of our certification programs through our virtual learning platform.

Our instructors bring a combined background that includes active and retired law enforcement officers, United States Marine Corps veterans, NRA-certified range safety officers, USCCA-certified instructors, and Florida K License holders. Every class is taught by professionals who have real-world experience — not just classroom theory.

Curriculum

Our Training Programs

USS Academy offers a structured six-tier training curriculum designed to take students from their very first time handling a firearm all the way through professional-level instructor certification.

Tier 1 — Beginner & Core Safety

$99 – $179

Florida CWP class, Basic Handgun Safety, First Time Shooter, Women's Intro, Home Defense Fundamentals.

Tier 2 — Skill Building

$279

Defensive Shooting, Draw and Fire, Speed and Accuracy, Dynamic Movement. For students with Tier 1 or equivalent.

Tier 3 — Tactical & Specialized

$349

Tactical Scenarios, Black Box, Rifle/Carbine, Low Light, Vehicle Defense, Active Threat, Close Quarters.

Tier 4 — Precision & Science

$399

Science of the Shot, Zero to Accurate. Ballistics, advanced trigger control, long-range accuracy.

Tier 5 — Night Vision Elite

$499

Night vision operations, IR laser systems, tactical movements in darkness. Equipment provided.

Tier 6 — Professional Development

$799+

Instructor certification, curriculum design, range safety, legal liability, business fundamentals.

All six tiers available at Central Florida. Tiers 1–2 available statewide. Tiers 3–6 at select locations — contact us to confirm.

Get Started

How to Train in Your Area

Getting started with USS Academy is straightforward no matter which part of Florida you call home. Our team will connect you with the nearest partner range facility and help you select the right course based on your experience level and goals.

For students outside of Florida, our virtual classroom options cover the lecture and legal education components. Complete the classroom portion online, then schedule your live-fire session at a Florida partner facility.

We respond to all inquiries within one business day and can typically schedule you into a class within one to two weeks.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Does USS Academy have a permanent training facility?

USS Academy operates through a network of partner ranges across Florida rather than a single fixed location. This model allows us to bring professional firearms training directly to communities throughout the state. Contact us to find the partner range nearest to your location.

Do I need to travel to Orlando for training?

Not necessarily. We regularly hold Tier 1 and Tier 2 classes at partner ranges in Tampa Bay, South Florida, Jacksonville, the Panhandle, and the Space Coast. Call 407-305-8335 to check availability in your region.

Can I take classes online from outside Florida?

Yes. USS Academy offers virtual classroom sessions covering lecture, legal education, and safety fundamentals. Available to students anywhere in the United States. Live-fire components completed at a Florida partner range or approved range in your home state.

How do I know which training tier is right for me?

New to firearms or getting your CWP? Start with Tier 1. Have your CWP and basic experience? Tier 2. Tiers 3–5 are for experienced shooters. Tier 6 is for aspiring instructors. Call 407-305-8335 for a personal recommendation.
