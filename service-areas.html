Why USS Academy Trains Statewide

USS Academy has been at the forefront of firearms education in Florida for over 27 years. What started as a local concealed weapons permit class in Central Florida has grown into one of the most recognized firearms training programs in the southeastern United States. With more than 29,000 students trained across every region of the state, USS Academy has built a reputation for delivering safe, professional, and accessible firearms instruction to civilians, law enforcement professionals, military personnel, and corporate security teams alike.

Our ability to serve students statewide comes from a growing network of partner ranges and training facilities across all of Florida's major metropolitan areas and rural communities. From the military-heavy Panhandle region near Pensacola and Eglin Air Force Base, through the rapidly growing Tampa Bay corridor, down to the densely populated South Florida metro that includes Miami, Fort Lauderdale, and West Palm Beach — USS Academy has established training relationships that allow us to bring our curriculum directly to the communities that need it most.

In addition to our in-person training at partner ranges across the state, USS Academy offers online and virtual classroom options that extend our reach beyond Florida's borders. Students from anywhere in the United States can access our foundational safety courses, concealed carry law education, and classroom-based portions of our certification programs through our virtual learning platform. This hybrid model means that no matter where you are located, you can begin your firearms education journey with USS Academy and complete your hands-on training at one of our Florida partner facilities when you are ready.

Our instructors bring a combined background that includes active and retired law enforcement officers, United States Marine Corps veterans, NRA-certified range safety officers, USCCA-certified instructors, and Florida K License holders. Every class is taught by professionals who have real-world experience carrying firearms in high-stakes environments — not just classroom theory. This depth of instructor experience is what separates USS Academy from weekend certification mills and ensures our students leave every course with genuine competence and confidence.

Our Training Programs

USS Academy offers a structured six-tier training curriculum designed to take students from their very first time handling a firearm all the way through professional-level instructor certification. Each tier builds on the skills developed in the previous level, creating a clear progression path for every student regardless of their starting point.

Tier 1 — Beginner and Core Safety

Price: $99 – $179

Our entry-level programs including the Florida Concealed Weapons Permit class, Basic Handgun Safety, First Time Shooter Experience, Women's Only Intro, and Home Defense Fundamentals. Perfect for anyone new to firearms or seeking their CWP certification.

Tier 2 — Skill Building and Development

Price: $279

Intermediate courses that build on your foundation. Includes Defensive Shooting Fundamentals, Draw and Fire drills, Speed and Accuracy development, and Dynamic Movement shooting. Designed for students who have completed Tier 1 or have equivalent experience.

Tier 3 — Tactical and Specialized

Price: $349

Advanced tactical training including Tactical Scenario Training, Black Box Simulation, Rifle and Carbine Fundamentals, Low Light Shooting, Vehicle Defense, Active Threat Response, and Close Quarters Combat. Real-world scenario-based training for serious practitioners.

Tier 4 — Precision and Science

Price: $399

Precision-focused courses including Science of the Shot and Zero to Accurate. These programs dive deep into ballistics, marksmanship fundamentals, trigger control at an advanced level, and long-range accuracy. Ideal for competitive shooters and those pursuing mastery.

Tier 5 — Night Vision Elite Tactical

Price: $499

Elite-level night vision and low-light operations training. Students learn to operate firearms systems under night vision devices, execute tactical movements in darkness, and engage targets with IR laser aiming systems. Equipment provided during the course.

Tier 6 — Professional Development

Price: $799+

Instructor certification and professional development programs for those looking to teach firearms courses professionally. Covers curriculum development, range safety officer responsibilities, student management, legal liability, and business fundamentals for independent instructors.

All six tiers are available at our Central Florida home base. Tier 1 and Tier 2 programs are available at partner ranges across all Florida regions listed above. Tier 3 through Tier 6 programs are available at select locations — contact us to confirm availability in your area.

How to Train With USS Academy in Your Area

Getting started with USS Academy is straightforward no matter which part of Florida you call home. Our team will connect you with the nearest partner range facility in your region and help you select the right course based on your experience level and goals. Whether you are a complete beginner looking to earn your Florida Concealed Weapons Permit or an experienced shooter seeking advanced tactical training, we have a program and a location that fits your needs.

For students outside of Florida or those who prefer to begin their training from home, our virtual classroom options cover the lecture and legal education components of many of our courses. You can complete the classroom portion online at your own pace and then schedule your live-fire range session at a Florida partner facility at a time that works for your schedule. This flexible approach is especially popular with students who travel to Florida seasonally or who want to prepare thoroughly before their first range visit.

To find classes in your area, schedule a private session, or ask questions about which course is right for you, reach out to our team directly. We respond to all inquiries within one business day and can typically schedule you into a class within one to two weeks of your initial contact.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does USS Academy have a permanent training facility I can visit?

USS Academy operates through a network of partner ranges across Florida rather than a single fixed location. This model allows us to bring professional firearms training directly to communities throughout the state. Our home base and highest class frequency is in the Central Florida and Greater Orlando area, where we hold weekly classes at multiple partner ranges. Contact us to find the partner range nearest to your location.

Do I need to travel to Orlando for training or can I take classes in my city?

Not necessarily. While our Central Florida locations offer the widest selection of classes and the most frequent scheduling, we regularly hold Tier 1 and Tier 2 classes at partner ranges in Tampa Bay, South Florida, Jacksonville, the Panhandle, and the Space Coast. If there is sufficient demand in your area, we can also arrange dedicated class dates at a local partner facility. Call 800-508-8772 to check the current schedule for your region.

Can I take USS Academy classes online if I live outside Florida?

Yes. USS Academy offers virtual classroom sessions that cover the lecture, legal education, and safety fundamentals portions of our curriculum. These online classes are available to students anywhere in the United States. If your state requires a live-fire component for permit certification, you will need to complete that portion in person at one of our Florida partner ranges or at an approved range in your home state. Our team can help you understand the requirements for your specific situation.

How do I know which training tier is right for me?

If you have never handled a firearm or are looking to get your Florida Concealed Weapons Permit, start with Tier 1. If you already have your CWP and basic range experience, Tier 2 will help you develop practical defensive shooting skills. Tiers 3 through 5 are designed for experienced shooters who want tactical, precision, or specialty training. Tier 6 is specifically for individuals pursuing a career in firearms instruction. If you are unsure, call us at 800-508-8772 and one of our instructors will recommend the right starting point based on your experience and goals.
