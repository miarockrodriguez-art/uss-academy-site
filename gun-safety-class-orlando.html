Firearm Safety & Handling

Gun Safety Class Orlando, FL

USS Academy's gun safety class in Orlando teaches the foundational handling, storage, and responsibility that every gun owner needs — whether the firearm in your home is for self-defense, for sport, or for the family's long-term safety.

Overview

The Gun Safety Class Every Owner Should Take

Safety is the foundation of responsible firearm ownership. USS Academy's gun safety class in Orlando is built for the gun owner who wants a real foundation — the handling, the storage, the legal framework, and the household awareness that keeps a firearm from becoming a liability. Over 27 years we have taught thousands of Orlando residents the safety fundamentals that prevent accidents and build confidence.

The class covers the four universal rules of firearm safety, safe handling techniques, secure storage options, transportation, and the legal and civil responsibility of owning a firearm in Florida. Every class is led by a certified instructor with operational experience — Law Enforcement Firearms Instructor credentials, U.S. Marine Corps, NRA, USCCA, and Florida Class K License backgrounds.

What You Learn

Safety Curriculum

The gun safety class is structured around the principle that every safety behavior must become automatic. If a safety rule requires thought in a crisis, it failed in training.

The Four Universal Rules

Treat every firearm as loaded. Never point at anything you do not intend to destroy. Keep finger off trigger until ready to fire. Know your target and what is beyond it.

Safe Handling

Loading, unloading, clearing, and verifying. Administrative handling vs live handling. Handling near other people.

Safe Storage

Gun safes, trigger locks, cable locks, ammunition storage, separate-storage principles, family-in-home considerations.

Transportation

Vehicle transport, open-carry vs concealed transport, travel between states, loaded vs unloaded in transit.

Legal & Civil Responsibility

Florida statutes on negligent discharge, child access prevention, and the civil liability that extends beyond criminal law.

Live-Fire Introduction

Optional range session with instructor-supervised live-fire for students who want to apply classroom concepts on the range.

Who It's For

New Owners, Families, and Everyone in Between

The gun safety class is designed for adults who own a firearm, are considering owning a firearm, or live in a household with a firearm. Many first-time gun owners take the class immediately after purchase. Many experienced shooters take the class years into ownership because they never had formal instruction. Families enroll together so the entire household understands the same rules.

If you are not yet a firearm owner but are thinking about it, the gun safety class is a useful step before purchase. If you own a firearm and have never had formal instruction, this class is a responsible upgrade to your household's safety posture.

Why Our Orlando Class

The Foundation We Insist On Teaching Right

There is no shortage of casual safety tutorials online. USS Academy's gun safety class exists because a casual tutorial is not enough — safety training needs structure, accountability, and the hands-on reinforcement that only an in-person class provides.

  • Certified instructors. LE Firearms Instructor, USMC, NRA, USCCA, Florida K License credentials.
  • Hands-on handling. Every student gets direct instruction, not a video and a handout.
  • Small class sizes. Capped enrollment so no student gets lost in the back of the room.
  • Family-friendly. Spouses, partners, and family members are welcome to enroll together.
  • Rental firearms available. Practice handling without needing to own yet.
  • Foundation for everything else. The gun safety class feeds directly into our concealed carry, defensive, and advanced programs.
Class Logistics

Where, When, and How

Location: Classes are held at USS Academy's Orlando-area partner range, 595 Donovan Crews Road, Saint Cloud, FL 34773. Classroom-only sessions can be hosted at additional venues on request.

Duration: Plan on 3 to 4 hours. Classes with a live-fire component run 4 to 5 hours.

Scheduling: Classes run weekly. See the calendar for live dates or request a private/group session for 6 or more students.

What to Bring: Valid photo ID. Your own handgun and ammunition if you have them. Rental firearms available on request. Eye and ear protection (range loaners available). Water, closed-toe shoes, sleeved shirt.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Who should take a gun safety class?

Every gun owner and every adult in a household with a firearm benefits from a structured gun safety class. New gun owners especially, but also experienced shooters who have never had formal instruction.

Is this a concealed carry class?

No. The gun safety class focuses on safe handling, storage, and responsibility. If you want a concealed carry permit, see our CWP Class Orlando.

Do I need to bring a firearm?

Rental firearms are available on request. If you own a handgun, you are welcome to bring it. Ammunition, eye and ear protection are available from the range.

How long is the class?

Most gun safety classes run about 3 to 4 hours depending on class size and whether live-fire is included.

Is this class good for a family?

Yes. Many families enroll together. Safe storage, family-in-home considerations, and age-appropriate education are covered throughout.

Do children take this class?

The gun safety class is designed for adults. For youth and family introductions, we offer dedicated programs on request. Call 407-305-8335.
