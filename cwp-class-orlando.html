The CWP Class Orlando Trusts

If you have made the decision to carry concealed in the state of Florida, the first step is real — not theoretical — training. USS Academy's CWP class in Orlando is the original training program that has prepared more than 29,000 Florida residents to legally carry a concealed firearm, confidently handle real scenarios, and pass the state-required proficiency standard.

This is not a classroom-only online checkbox. This is not a "shoot-one-round-and-sign-here" rubber-stamp course. USS Academy's CWP training is delivered by certified Law Enforcement Firearms Instructors, U.S. Marine Corps instructors, NRA Instructors, USCCA Instructors, and Florida Class K Instructors. Every class ends with a state-approved Certificate of Completion that meets the requirement under Florida Statute 790.06(2)(h).

The USS Academy Standard

Every USS Academy student trains with real firearms — their own or ours — at licensed partner ranges under certified Law Enforcement Firearms Instructors with real military and law enforcement experience. No simulations. No shortcuts. No parking lot bucket shots. No toy guns. Just real training that prepares you for real situations.

What the CWP Class Covers

Florida's concealed weapons permit requirement is clear on paper but has real implications the moment you carry live. USS Academy's Orlando CWP class is built to give you both: legal knowledge and physical capability. In one training day you walk through:

  • Florida law — Statute 790 (firearms), Statute 776 (justifiable use of force), reciprocity, carry zones, prohibited places, stand your ground, castle doctrine.
  • Firearm safety — the four universal rules, safe storage, vehicle transport, family-in-home considerations.
  • Mechanics and handling — loading, unloading, grip, stance, sight alignment, sight picture, trigger control, reholstering.
  • Live-fire qualification — range safety brief, instructor-supervised live-fire, state-required minimum competency demonstration, target proof.
  • Home defense, scenario awareness, and de-escalation — real-world thinking, not TV thinking.
  • Application roadmap — how to submit your FDACS permit application once your Certificate of Completion is in hand.

Who This Class Is For

If you are a Florida resident (or eligible non-resident) 21 or older with the legal right to possess a firearm, the CWP class is the correct starting point. USS Academy's class is built for every learner — from first-time gun owners who have never held a pistol, to seasoned shooters who need the certified course to complete their application, to military veterans and law-enforcement officers whose backgrounds already give them handling skill but who want the formal state requirement met.

We specifically welcome women stepping into training for the first time, and we regularly run sessions with a majority of female students led by instructors who understand that learning to carry is a personal — often deeply emotional — decision, not just a transaction.

Orlando Class Logistics

Location

Classes are held at USS Academy's primary partner range, 595 Donovan Crews Road, Saint Cloud, FL 34773 — approximately 30 minutes south of downtown Orlando. Additional Orlando-area partner ranges are activated seasonally for capacity. See the calendar for live dates, or view our broader service areas across Florida.

What to Bring

  • Valid photo ID
  • Your own handgun and ammunition (rental firearms available — contact us ahead)
  • Eye and ear protection (range loaners available)
  • Water bottle, closed-toe shoes, sleeved shirt

Class Duration

Plan on a full half-day experience. Most CWP classes run 4 to 5 hours including classroom instruction, range safety brief, live-fire qualification, and certificate issuance.

Certificate and Next Steps

Every student who successfully completes the class receives a state-approved Certificate of Completion the same day. Use it to complete your Florida Concealed Weapons Permit application with FDACS or a county tax collector's office. USS Academy's instructors will walk you through the exact submission steps at the end of class.

Florida CWP Requirements — Short Version

You must be 21 (or 18 with qualifying military service), a U.S. citizen or lawful permanent resident, legally able to possess a firearm, and able to demonstrate competency with a firearm through a certified training course with live-fire. Your Certificate of Completion from USS Academy is accepted by FDACS across all 67 Florida counties.

For the full official FDACS requirements, visit fdacs.gov. Florida statutes of interest are Chapter 790 and Chapter 776 — USS Academy's class covers both in plain English.
