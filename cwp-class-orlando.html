Overview

The CWP Class Orlando Trusts

If you have made the decision to carry concealed in Florida, the first step is real training - not a classroom-only online checkbox and not a shoot-one-round-and-sign-here rubber-stamp course.

USS Academy's CWP class is the original training program that has prepared more than 29,000 Florida residents to legally carry a concealed firearm, confidently handle real scenarios, and pass the state-required proficiency standard. Every class is taught in person, includes live-fire range work, and ends with a state-approved Certificate of Completion that meets the requirement under Florida Statute 790.06(2)(h).

Training is delivered by certified Law Enforcement Firearms Instructors, U.S. Marine Corps instructors, NRA Instructors, USCCA Instructors, and Florida Class K Instructors. Students leave the range knowing how to load, draw, aim, fire, clear malfunctions, reholster, and understand the legal weight of every decision they make while carrying a firearm under Florida law.

Logistics

Orlando Class Details

Here is what to expect on class day and what to bring with you.

Location

Classes are held at USS Academy's primary partner range, 595 Donovan Crews Road, Saint Cloud, FL 34773 - approximately 30 minutes south of downtown Orlando. Additional Orlando-area partner ranges are activated seasonally for capacity. See the calendar for live dates or view our broader service areas across Florida.

Duration

Plan on a half-day experience. Most CWP classes run 4 to 5 hours including classroom instruction, range safety brief, live-fire qualification, and certificate issuance.

What to Bring

Valid photo ID. Your own handgun and ammunition (rental available on request). Eye and ear protection (range loaners available). Water, closed-toe shoes, sleeved shirt.

Certificate

Every student who successfully completes the class receives a state-approved Certificate of Completion the same day. Use it to submit your Florida CWP application through FDACS or a county tax collector's office. Instructors walk you through submission at the end of class.

Florida CWP Requirements

You must be 21 (or 18 with qualifying military service), a U.S. citizen or lawful permanent resident, legally able to possess a firearm, and able to demonstrate competency with a firearm through a certified training course with live-fire. Your Certificate of Completion from USS Academy is accepted by FDACS across all 67 Florida counties. For the full official requirements, visit fdacs.gov. The relevant Florida statutes are Chapter 790 and Chapter 776, both of which are covered in plain English during the classroom session.

Questions

CWP Class FAQ

How long is the CWP class?

Plan on a half-day experience. Most USS Academy CWP classes run 4 to 5 hours including classroom instruction, range safety brief, live-fire qualification, and certificate issuance.

Do I need to bring my own firearm?

You may bring your own handgun and ammunition, or contact us ahead of class to arrange a rental firearm. Eye and ear protection are also available from the range if you do not have your own.

What does the CWP certificate allow me to do?

Your state-approved Certificate of Completion satisfies the training requirement under Florida Statute 790.06(2)(h) and is used to submit your Florida Concealed Weapons Permit application with the Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services or a county tax collector's office.

Are USS Academy instructors certified?

Yes. USS Academy instructors hold credentials from the NRA, USCCA, the United States Marine Corps, Florida Class K License, and Law Enforcement Firearms Instructor programs. All instructors are state-certified and bring real-world operational experience.

Can I take the class with a friend or group?

Yes. Many students enroll with family, friends, or workplace colleagues. Group pricing applies at 6 or more students. For private group classes we can schedule a dedicated session on request.

How far in advance should I book?

Classes run weekly and fill quickly. Booking 1 to 2 weeks ahead is recommended. Last-minute seats are occasionally available - call 407-305-8335 to check.
