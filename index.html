The Science of the Shot — Respiratory Pause, Trigger Control & Precision Marksmanship

True precision shooting begins where most training stops. USS Academy's Precision Marksmanship course teaches the science behind every accurate shot — including the respiratory pause, the brief moment of natural stillness between exhale and inhale when the body is most stable and the shot breaks cleanest. This course covers the full fundamentals of precision: respiratory control and the natural pause cycle, trigger discipline and break timing, natural point of aim (NPOA), follow-through, and reading your own body's mechanics to eliminate error at the source. Applicable to pistol, rifle, and long-range platforms. Ideal for competitive shooters, law enforcement precision teams, military marksmen, and any student who wants to understand not just how to shoot — but why a shot is accurate. This is the science behind the shot.

27

Zero to Accurate — Sight Alignment, Sight Picture & Zeroing Fundamentals

Before you can shoot accurately, your firearm has to be set up accurately. USS Academy's Zero to Accurate course teaches students how to properly align their sights and zero their firearm so that point of aim equals point of impact — the foundation of every accurate shot. This course covers the two critical fundamentals: sight alignment (the correct relationship between front and rear sights) and sight picture (placing that aligned sight on the target correctly). Students then learn zeroing procedures for iron sights, red dot optics, and magnified scopes — including proper distance selection, adjustments, and confirmation drills. Whether you're mounting your first optic, setting up a new rifle, or correcting an existing zero, this course ensures your equipment is performing exactly as it should before you ever engage a target. Applicable to pistols, rifles, and shotguns across all optic platforms.

Before You Arrive

What to Bring — Range Preparation

Come prepared and ready to train. Review this checklist before every range session or class.

Mandatory Items
◆ Eye Protection — ANSI-rated shooting glasses required
◆ Ear Protection — Over-ear muffs or in-ear plugs (electronic muffs recommended)
◆ Photo ID — Valid government-issued identification
◆ Closed-Toe Shoes — No sandals, flip-flops, or open-toe footwear
◆ Range-Appropriate Attire — No low-cut tops, high collars recommended to prevent hot brass contact
Bring Your Own
◆ Firearm — Your personal firearm (if applicable to course). Unloaded, cased.
◆ Ammunition — Factory-loaded ammo appropriate for your firearm. Check course requirements.
◆ Targets — Standard silhouette or bullseye targets (some courses supply these)
◆ Water & Snacks — Stay hydrated, especially for outdoor range sessions in Florida heat
◆ Notepad & Pen — For notes during classroom portions

Note: Some items may be supplied by USS Academy depending on the course. CWP classes include all required materials. Check your confirmation email for course-specific requirements. Questions? Call 800-508-8772.

USS Academy Competitive Shooting

Join Our Competitive Shooting Group

USS Academy's competitive shooting group is open to all skill levels — from local club newcomers to those preparing for internationally sanctioned matches. Choose the tier that fits your goals and let us help you compete at your highest level.

TIER 1
Local Club Matches

Club Competitor

Built for shooters who want to experience the energy and discipline of competitive shooting at the local level. USS Academy's Tier 1 group participates in local club matches — a great introduction to organized competition in a fun, low-pressure environment. Sharpen your fundamentals, build consistency under match conditions, and become part of a community of serious shooters.

▸Local USPSA, IDPA, and Steel Challenge club matches
▸Match preparation training sessions with USS Academy instructors
▸Equipment guidance and stage-reading coaching
▸Group match attendance and team community
▸Open to all experience levels — beginners welcome
TIER 2
Club to International

Sanctioned Match Competitor

For the serious competitor. Tier 2 takes you from local club matches all the way to internationally sanctioned competition. USS Academy prepares Tier 2 members for the full competitive pipeline — structured training, match simulation, performance analysis, and tactical preparation for major events including Tactical Games 2027 and 2028.

▸All Tier 1 benefits included
▸Structured training pathway for USPSA, IDPA & sanctioned matches
▸Performance analysis, video review & personalized coaching
▸Tactical Games preparation — 2027 & 2028 competition cycles
▸International sanctioned match travel coordination
▸Intermediate–advanced competitors only

Tactical Games Preparation — 2027 & 2028

The Tactical Games are one of the most demanding multi-discipline competitive shooting events in the world — combining firearms proficiency with physical fitness challenges under real match pressure. USS Academy is building a preparation track specifically for Tier 2 members who want to compete in the 2027 and 2028 Tactical Games cycles. Training covers shooting accuracy under physical stress, stage strategy, equipment selection, and the conditioning required to perform at the highest level when it counts.
