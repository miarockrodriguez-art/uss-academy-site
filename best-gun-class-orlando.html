Classes We Offer

USS Academy offers a full range of firearms training classes designed to take you from absolute beginner to advanced tactical shooter. Whatever your goals are, we have a class for you:

  • CWP / CCW Concealed Carry Class — The class required to apply for your Florida Concealed Weapons Permit. Includes classroom instruction, hands-on training, live-fire qualification, and your certificate.
  • Beginner Firearms Course — Designed for first-time gun owners and people who have never fired a weapon. Covers safety fundamentals, basic marksmanship, and hands-on range time.
  • Self-Defense Firearms Training — Focused on real-world self-defense scenarios, situational awareness, and defensive shooting techniques.
  • G-License Security Training — Professional security guard training for those pursuing a Florida G License (armed security).
  • Night Vision Tactical Course — Elite-level training using night vision optics and low-light shooting techniques. One of the most advanced courses available in Central Florida.
  • Tactical Firearms Training — Advanced tactical shooting including movement, cover and concealment, multiple targets, and stress-fire drills.
  • Competitive Shooting Prep — Training for USPSA, IDPA, and other competitive shooting disciplines. Learn competition rules, stage strategy, and speed techniques.
  • Ladies Day (Ember & Iron) — A ladies-only shooting event designed for women who want to learn in a comfortable, supportive, all-female environment.
  • Private Lessons — One-on-one instruction tailored to your specific goals, skill level, and timeline. Available by appointment.
