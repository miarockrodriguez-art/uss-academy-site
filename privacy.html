Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

USS Academy LLC ("USS Academy," "we," "us") collects the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, date of birth, state-issued ID number when provided for class registration, waiver signing, or certificate processing.
  • Payment Information: Credit card and billing information processed through our secure payment processors (Stripe, Square). We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage Information: Website browsing data, IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs collected through standard web analytics.
  • Communications: Records of emails, SMS messages, phone calls, and form submissions you send to or receive from us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process class registrations and payments
  • Issue training certificates and track permit renewals
  • Send booking confirmations, reminders, and follow-up communications
  • Provide customer support
  • Send marketing communications about classes, events, and promotions (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations including Florida firearms training documentation requirements
  • Improve our services and website

3. SMS and Text Message Policy

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive text messages from USS Academy including:

  • Booking confirmations and reminders
  • Class updates and schedule changes
  • Promotional offers and new class announcements
  • Certificate and renewal reminders

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from marketing messages. Reply HELP for assistance. We honor all opt-out requests within 10 business days as required by the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA).

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, scheduling platforms, CRM systems, and communication tools that help us operate our business.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Business Partners: Range facilities where classes are held (limited to necessary operational information only).

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS), secure payment processing, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

  • Training records and certificates: Retained indefinitely as required for permit documentation.
  • Contact information: Retained for the duration of our business relationship and up to 4 years after last contact for compliance purposes.
  • Payment records: Retained as required by tax and financial regulations.
  • Marketing consent records: Retained for 4 years as required by TCPA.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@ussacademy.org or call 800-508-8772.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, analyze traffic, and for marketing purposes. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. Minors may participate in certain programs with parental/guardian consent and supervision as outlined in our waiver.

11. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
