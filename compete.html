Official Team

USS Elite Competitive Team

USS Academy's competitive shooting team representing Central Florida in USPSA, IDPA, and regional competitions across the Southeast.

The USS Elite Competitive Team is the official competition arm of USS Academy. Our team members train under USS Academy's advanced coaching program and compete as representatives of the academy at sanctioned events throughout Florida, the Southeast, and nationally.

▸Official USS Academy team jersey and competition gear
▸USPSA, IDPA & Steel Challenge team representation
▸Dedicated coaching, match prep, and performance tracking
▸Regional and national match travel coordination
▸Selection by invitation or application — intermediate to advanced
