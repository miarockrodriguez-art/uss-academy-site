Concealed Carry Class Kissimmee, FL

The concealed carry class Kissimmee and Osceola County residents have relied on for 27 years. Overview

The Concealed Carry Class for Kissimmee and Osceola County

Kissimmee has grown into one of Central Florida's most active residential corridors, and with that growth has come a consistent demand for professional concealed carry training taught by people who understand the responsibility of what they teach. USS Academy has been the concealed carry class of record for Kissimmee, St. Cloud, Poinciana, and the surrounding Osceola County communities since 1997 — 29,000+ students trained across 27 years.

Every USS Academy concealed carry class is taught in person by a certified Law Enforcement Firearms Instructor. Every class includes live-fire qualification on a licensed partner range that sits inside Osceola County at 595 Donovan Crews Road, Saint Cloud — a short 15 to 20 minute drive from most Kissimmee addresses. Every student leaves with a state-approved Certificate of Completion the same day, accepted by the Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services across all 67 counties.

Class Curriculum

Classroom and Live-Fire Qualification

The concealed carry class covers Florida's legal framework, firearm safety and handling, live-fire qualification, and the practical thinking required to carry responsibly.

Florida Law

Statute 790, Statute 776 justifiable use of force, reciprocity, carry zones, prohibited places, stand your ground, castle doctrine.

Firearm Safety

Four universal rules, safe storage, vehicle transport, family-in-home considerations.

Handling & Mechanics

Grip, stance, sight alignment, trigger control, reholstering, malfunction clearing.

Live-Fire Qualification

Range safety brief, instructor-supervised live-fire, state-required minimum competency.

Scenario Awareness

Home defense, public carry thinking, decision-making under stress.

Permit Application

How to submit your Florida concealed weapons license application through FDACS or a county tax collector's office.

Travel From Kissimmee

A Short Drive to Your Class

USS Academy's primary partner range is located at 595 Donovan Crews Road, Saint Cloud, FL 34773. From Kissimmee, the drive is typically 15 to 20 minutes via Neptune Road, US-192, or the Florida Turnpike depending on traffic and your starting address. Students from Poinciana, Buenaventura Lakes, Celebration, and Four Corners take similar routes.

For Kissimmee area groups of 10 or more students, USS Academy can host private classes at Kissimmee venues including hotel conference rooms, HOA clubhouses, and community centers. See our Host a Class program for hosted session inquiries.

Who Takes This Class

Built for Every Student

The concealed carry class is built for every learner. First-time shooters enroll regularly and are taught patiently from grip and stance. Experienced gun owners enroll because they need the formal state-required certificate for their Florida CWL application. Military veterans and law enforcement officers enroll to meet Florida's specific certification requirement. Women make up a significant portion of our students, and we run select sessions under female-led instruction.

Families enroll together, couples enroll together, and friend groups and workplace colleagues regularly share a class. Group pricing applies at 6 or more students, and private group sessions can be scheduled for booking inquiries of 10 or more.

Class Logistics

What to Expect on Class Day

Location: 595 Donovan Crews Road, Saint Cloud, FL 34773 — 15 to 20 minutes from most Kissimmee addresses.

Duration: Plan on 4 to 5 hours including classroom, safety brief, live-fire qualification, and certificate issuance.

What to Bring: Valid photo ID. Your own handgun and ammunition (rental available on request). Eye and ear protection (range loaners available). Water, closed-toe shoes, sleeved shirt.

Certificate: Every student who successfully completes the class receives a state-approved Certificate of Completion the same day.

Scheduling: Classes run weekly. See the calendar for the next available date or call 407-305-8335.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is USS Academy's class location from Kissimmee?

Our primary partner range is in Saint Cloud, approximately 15 to 20 minutes from most Kissimmee addresses via the 192 or Neptune Road.

Does this class qualify for the Florida CWL?

Yes. The class meets Florida Statute 790.06(2)(h) and the state-approved Certificate of Completion is accepted by FDACS across all 67 counties.

Do you offer group pricing for Kissimmee families and friends?

Yes. Group pricing applies at 6 or more students. Many students enroll with family, friends, or workplace colleagues.

Can the class be hosted at a venue in Kissimmee?

Yes. Private group classes can be scheduled at Kissimmee venues for bookings of 10 or more students. See our Host a Class program.

I've never shot a gun. Is this class for me?

Yes. The class is designed to welcome first-time shooters alongside experienced students. Instructors teach patiently from grip and stance.

Are rental firearms available?

Yes. Call ahead and we arrange a rental handgun and ammunition for class day.
