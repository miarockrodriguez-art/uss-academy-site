Overview

Additional partner ranges across the region are activated seasonally based on demand. Every class ends with a state-approved Certificate of Completion issued the same day.

Florida CCW Terminology

Understanding CCW, CWP, and CWL

If you are searching for a Central Florida CCW class, you may have encountered three different abbreviations: CCW (Concealed Carry Weapon), CWP (Concealed Weapons Permit), and CWL (Concealed Weapons License). In Florida, all three refer to the same license — the state permit that allows you to legally carry a concealed firearm. The Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services (FDACS) officially calls it the Concealed Weapons License.

USS Academy uses CCW, CWP, and CWL interchangeably throughout our materials because students search using different terms. The class we teach is one class, meeting one state requirement, issuing one certificate that FDACS accepts for the Concealed Weapons License application — regardless of which abbreviation you searched to find us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does a CCW class from Central Florida qualify me statewide?

Yes. The Florida Concealed Weapons License is a state license, not a regional one. A CCW class completed in Central Florida satisfies the state training requirement and is accepted by FDACS statewide.

What counties does your Central Florida class serve?

Our primary class location in Saint Cloud is convenient to Orange, Osceola, Seminole, Lake, Volusia, and Brevard counties. Students regularly drive from as far as Polk and Brevard.

Is this different from the CWP class?

No. In Florida, CCW and CWP refer to the same permit — the Concealed Weapons License. We use both terms throughout the site.

Are there multiple class locations in Central Florida?

Our primary training location is in Saint Cloud. We also activate partner ranges across Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Lake, and Volusia counties seasonally.

Do you offer a Central Florida group discount?

Yes. Group pricing applies at 6 or more students — ideal for families, workplace teams, or community groups enrolling together.

What experience do your Central Florida instructors have?

Our instructors hold credentials from the U.S. Marine Corps, NRA, USCCA, Florida Class K License, and Law Enforcement Firearms Instructor programs. Every one has operational field experience backing up the credential.
